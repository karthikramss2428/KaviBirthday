Caring for others comes naturally here, and that same light is seen in you. When someone is sad, your heart quietly bends toward them. If anyone needs help, you stand beside them without any hesitation, and that makes you shine so much more.

When the future appears in the mind, it is always with you standing there. There is a wish to grow with you, care for you, and stand beside you through every high and low. You are not just love; you feel like home.

You are the safe place where this heart feels calm and protected. With you, there is no need to pretend or be strong all the time. You are peace, comfort, and the one space where everything finally feels okay.
